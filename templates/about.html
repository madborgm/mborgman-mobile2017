Accountability: About This Data Set

Where did this data come from?

All of my data came from tracking myself for three weeks through the AWARE framework on my phone. The data was pushed to a database on Google Cloud's Platform.

I used Google Maps to visualize where many of the latitude/longitude data instances were on a map. I used Underground Weather to research what the weather was on the days I was walking to class.

Who has access to it?

My tables are all public through Google Fusion tables. However, the large data set is hosted on my Google Cloud Database.
